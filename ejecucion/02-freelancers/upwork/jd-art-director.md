**Title:** AI Art Director — Visual System + AI Image Pipeline for Premium Fitness Brand (8 weeks)

**Overview:**
We are rebuilding the website of Mexico's leading premium fitness chain (49 clubs, ~148
pages). Declared goal: the best fitness website in Mexico, benchmarked monthly against three
international references. We need an art director who owns the complete visual system AND an
AI image pipeline: real reference photography → AI generation → style transfer and upscaling
(Topaz / DaVinci Resolve APIs) → human curation. Every image that ships passes through your
eye; nothing publishes without your approval.

**Responsibilities:**
- Define the full visual system (W1): tokens, typography, palette, grid, components, and the
  art direction of the home, club-page, hub, class and membership templates
- Design and operate the AI image pipeline from the client's reference photos, applying style
  and upscale via Topaz/DaVinci, and curate every published asset
- Sign the pillar gate (W2): your signature certifies the template scales to 148 pages
  without degrading
- Audit each generated batch visually by sampling (club pages, hubs, classes)
- Keep assets within performance budgets (an image that breaks Core Web Vitals doesn't ship)

**Deliverables by milestone:**
- M1 (W1): visual system + templates v1 delivered to implementation
- M2 (W2): pillar gate signed (home + 1 club page + 1 hub approved)
- M3 (W3–W4): curated image library for the 49-club-page batch and hubs
- M4 (W5–W6): curated assets for class pages; visual sign-off of the completeness gate

**Required qualifications:**
- Exceptional art-direction portfolio (brand-led digital products or campaigns) — this is the
  primary filter
- Real production experience with generative image tools AND a disciplined curation workflow
  (reference-driven, brand-consistent, no "AI look")
- Hands-on with Topaz and/or DaVinci Resolve for style/upscale finishing
- Experience building design systems that developers implement
- Spanish (the team operates in Mexican Spanish)

**Nice to have:** sports/fitness/lifestyle photography direction; motion/video finishing.

**Experience level:** Expert
**Project length & commitment:** 8 weeks; heavier at the start (15–20 h/week W1–W2, then 8–10)
**Budget type:** Fixed-price, one funded milestone per gate (amounts set in the posting)

**Screening questions:**
1. Link the 3 strongest visual systems or campaigns you directed. What was your exact role?
2. Show one example where you took AI-generated imagery to true brand quality. Describe the
   pipeline step by step (tools, reference handling, finishing).
3. How do you keep 148 pages of imagery consistent when a generator produces the variants?
4. Availability for the next 8 weeks and overlap with Mexico City hours?
