**Title:** Senior SEO Specialist (Mexico, native Spanish) — Content Strategy + Editorial QA for 148-page Rebuild

**Overview:**
We are rebuilding the website of Mexico's leading premium fitness chain (49 clubs, ~148 pages:
club pages with local SEO, 54 class pages, category hubs, memberships, blog). Content drafts
and meta tags are produced by AI from your strategy; you decide WHAT gets written, for WHICH
search intent, and whether the output meets the editorial bar in Mexican Spanish. You also own
the URL migration (301 map) of the existing site.

**Responsibilities:**
- Define the keyword and search-intent strategy per page type (club/local, class, hub,
  membership, blog) before mass production starts
- Co-sign the pillar gate: validate that the editorial template (heading hierarchy, content
  blocks, schema, interlinking) scales to the whole site
- Editorial review by sampling (≥20% of every AI-generated batch): tone of voice, es-MX,
  factual accuracy against the client's catalog — zero invented data
- Own the 54 class pages + blog batch as its curator of record
- Build and validate the 301 redirect map for the URL migration at final QA

**Deliverables by milestone:**
- M1 (W2): keyword strategy per page type, integrated into production templates
- M2 (W2): pillar gate editorial signature
- M3 (W4): editorial sign-off of the 50% gate (club pages + hubs sampled)
- M4 (W5): 54 class pages + blog batch curated and approved
- M5 (W7): 301 redirect map validated for go-live

**Required qualifications:**
- 6+ years SEO with demonstrable organic-growth cases in the Mexican market (show data)
- Native/naturally fluent Mexican Spanish and a sharp editorial eye
- Local SEO for multi-location businesses (Google Business Profile, local landing pages)
- Technical SEO literacy: schema.org, interlinking, migrations/301s, CWV awareness
- Comfort reviewing AI-drafted content at scale (sampling methods, quality rubrics)

**Nice to have:** fitness/wellness or membership-business experience; GA4/Search Console
reporting fluency (you'll define what the funnel dashboard must show, not build it).

**Experience level:** Expert
**Project length & commitment:** 8 weeks, 15–20 h/week
**Budget type:** Fixed-price, one funded milestone per gate (amounts set in the posting)

**Screening questions:**
1. Share 2 Mexican-market SEO cases with before/after organic data. What exactly did you do?
2. How would you structure search intent for a gym chain's site: 49 location pages vs 54
   class pages vs category hubs? Give a concrete example of each.
3. Describe your sampling method to QA a 50-page AI-written batch without reading all of it.
4. Have you run a site migration with 301s? What was the traffic impact at 90 days?
