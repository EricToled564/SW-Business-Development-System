**Title:** AI Instructional Designer (Native Spanish) — Sales Academy for 200 Advisors (10 weeks)

**Overview:**
Mexico's leading premium fitness chain (49 clubs) is launching an internal sales academy for
its 200 commercial advisors. Phase 1: interactive learning modules teaching a consultative
sales methodology built on a documented customer-motivation taxonomy. Phase 2: weekly AI
voice role-play practice against simulated customers (built by a separate engineer on an
existing conversational engine). You own the instructional design of Phase 1 and the
pedagogical standard of the whole program. Production is AI-first: AI drafts modules, scripts
and assessments from your design; you architect the learning experience, run content
certification with the client's sales experts, and approve what ships.

**Responsibilities:**
- Turn the sales methodology and motivation taxonomy into a modular interactive curriculum:
  learning objectives, sequencing, micro-lessons, exercises, assessments
- Direct AI-assisted production of all modules and curate every one before release
- Design and run the content-certification process with the client's subject-matter experts —
  nothing publishes uncertified
- Author the evaluation rubrics and scenario scripts that the Phase-2 voice role-play system
  will use for automated scoring (formal handoff to the voice engineer)
- Define the learning-measurement plan (adoption, mastery, practice adherence KPIs)

**Deliverables by milestone:**
- M1: curriculum map and module architecture approved by client experts
- M2: Phase-1 interactive modules produced, certified and live for 200 advisors
- M3: certified evaluation rubrics + scenario scripts handed off to the role-play engineer
- M4: learning-measurement plan and pilot readout

**Required qualifications:**
- 6+ years instructional design for corporate training, ideally sales enablement
- Native/naturally fluent Mexican Spanish (all content ships in es-MX)
- Demonstrated AI-assisted course production (50–70% cycle-time reduction cases are the
  industry norm — show us yours)
- Assessment and rubric design experience (your rubrics feed automated scoring)
- SME-certification workflow experience (getting expert sign-off at scale)

**Nice to have:** fitness/wellness or membership-sales domain; role-play or simulation-based
training design; LMS/analytics literacy.

**Experience level:** Expert
**Project length & commitment:** 10 weeks, 15–20 h/week
**Budget type:** Fixed-price, one funded milestone per phase (amounts set in the posting)

**Screening questions:**
1. Share a corporate curriculum you designed end-to-end. What measurable outcome did it move?
2. Describe your AI-assisted production workflow: what the AI drafts, what only you decide,
   and how you QA at scale.
3. Have you designed rubrics consumed by an automated or simulated scoring system? Explain.
4. How do you run SME certification so it doesn't become the bottleneck of the project?
