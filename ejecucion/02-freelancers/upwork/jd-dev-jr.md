**Title:** Frontend Developer with Exceptional Design Eye — Premium Fitness Website (8 weeks)

**Overview:**
We are rebuilding the website of Mexico's leading premium fitness chain (~148 pages) with an
AI-first pipeline: AI generates page batches from approved templates; humans specify, curate
and polish. We need a frontend developer whose superpower is **design fidelity** — making the
implemented UI match the approved art direction pixel-perfect across breakpoints. You will
work under a senior Tech Lead and alongside an AI Art Director. We hire for design taste and
craft, not code volume (the AI writes most of the boilerplate).

**Responsibilities:**
- Implement and polish design-system components and page templates (tokens, typography,
  spacing, hover/focus states, motion)
- Correct AI-generated page batches wherever they drift from the approved design
- Fix visual and accessibility findings from each quality gate
- Verify responsive behavior and pixel-fidelity against templates on real devices

**Deliverables by milestone:**
- M1 (W1–W2): design-system components + templates implemented for the pillar gate
- M2 (W3–W4): visual corrections across the 49-club-page and hubs batches
- M3 (W5–W6): class pages batch polish
- M4 (W7): all visual/accessibility QA findings resolved

**Required qualifications:**
- 2–4 years frontend (HTML/CSS/JS, modern framework); an outstanding visual portfolio is the
  main filter — send only your best-designed work
- Strong CSS craft: layout, fluid type, animation, states
- Accessibility basics (contrast, focus, semantics) — our gates enforce WCAG 2.2 AA
- Git/PR workflow discipline
- Spanish (the team operates in Mexican Spanish)

**Nice to have:** design-tool fluency (Figma), experience polishing AI-generated code.

**Experience level:** Intermediate
**Project length & commitment:** 8 weeks, 20–30 h/week
**Budget type:** Fixed-price, one funded milestone per gate (amounts set in the posting)

**Screening questions:**
1. Share the 3 best-designed interfaces you have personally implemented (links, not mockups).
   What did you build in each?
2. A generated page is functionally correct but "feels off" versus the approved design. Walk
   us through how you detect and fix that.
3. What is your weekly availability for the next 8 weeks and overlap with Mexico City hours?
