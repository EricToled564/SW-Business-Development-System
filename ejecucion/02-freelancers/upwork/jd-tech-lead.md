**Title:** Tech Lead / Senior UX Developer — AI-First Website Rebuild (148 pages, 8 weeks)

**Overview:**
We are rebuilding the complete website of Mexico's leading premium fitness chain (49 clubs):
~148 pages including 49 club pages, 54 class pages, category hubs and membership flows, plus
an AI-powered questionnaire, a conversational agent and CRM integration. Production is
AI-first: Claude Code generates page batches from approved templates; a small senior team
writes the specs, curates the output and signs quality gates. You will be the technical owner
of the entire pipeline — the closest role to full-time on the project. Declared goal: the
best fitness website in Mexico.

**Responsibilities:**
- Own the repository, CI and an automated quality budget that blocks any red PR
  (Lighthouse ≥ 95, Core Web Vitals green, WCAG 2.2 AA, valid schema, 0 broken links)
- Orchestrate AI production sessions: templates, reusable skills, batch generation of 49 club
  pages, hubs and profiles from the client's real catalog
- Extract and structure the knowledge base (clubs/classes catalog)
- Direct one junior frontend developer (assignments and PR reviews)
- Sign the technical and QA gates (50%, Completeness, final QA, sign-off) and execute go-live
  with 301 redirects and monitoring

**Deliverables by milestone:**
- M1 (W1): repo + CI + quality budget live; knowledge base extracted
- M2 (W2): pillar gate — home + 1 club page + 1 hub approved on templates, pipelines ready
- M3 (W4): 50% gate — 49 club pages + hubs/profiles live, lead capture working end-to-end
- M4 (W6): completeness gate — all 148 pages + full funnel
- M5 (W8): final QA signed, go-live with redirects, training and monitoring

**Required qualifications:**
- 7+ years front-end/full-stack; portfolio of shipped, design-heavy marketing sites
- Proven hands-on experience driving AI coding tools (Claude Code, Cursor or similar) in
  real production, not experiments
- Deep Core Web Vitals / Lighthouse / accessibility (WCAG 2.2) expertise
- CI/CD ownership on GitHub; strict PR-based workflow
- Working Spanish (the site and the team operate in Mexican Spanish)

**Nice to have:** local SEO/schema experience; experience leading tiny senior teams;
fitness/retail multi-location sites.

**Experience level:** Expert
**Project length & commitment:** 8 weeks, 30–40 h/week
**Budget type:** Fixed-price, one funded milestone per gate (amounts set in the posting)

**Screening questions:**
1. Link 2–3 production sites you led where you owned performance budgets. What were the final
   Lighthouse/CWV numbers?
2. Describe a real workflow where an AI coding agent generated batches of pages/components
   under your specs. What did you automate and what did you keep human?
3. How do you make a 49-page batch keep WCAG 2.2 AA without reviewing every page by hand?
4. What is your availability for the next 8 weeks and your time zone overlap with Mexico City?
